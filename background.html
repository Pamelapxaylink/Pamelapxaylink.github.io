I am Laotian and Vietnamese.

My father is vietnamese and my mother is laotian. I grew up as a christian from my mother's side, and later on I experienced more of my buddhist religion from my father's side. This picture shows my friend and I at the temple celebrating the new years that happens every year around April. Around this time we count our blessings and show our gratitude to buddha. The outfit that I am wearing is called a "sin" in Lao and if you want to try out some of our delicious food, Sabaidee restaurant on Herndon is a great place to start.
