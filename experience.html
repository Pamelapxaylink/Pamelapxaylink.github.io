I am a monitor technician at Saint Agnes Medical Center. I work pms to night shifts and on the clock we catch any abnormalities that the patient's heart could be experiencing. We have to understand the medications the nurses administer to the patients as well as all of various heart rhythms we see. A normal day at work would consist of various interactions with medical staff upon admiting patients and their current state of condition. Somedays I will call a code blue (lethal unresponsive patient) and everyone rushes in to revive the patient. On less hectic days we all try to have a good shift and adapt to all of the changes going on right now especially with the pandemic. I love what I do, and if you have any questions regarding our hospital and what's going on with covid please feel free to contact me and where you can get tested. Don't forget to get your flu shots. Stay safe and healthy!
