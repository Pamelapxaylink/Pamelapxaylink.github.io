What does my name stand for?

I get this question a lot actually because of how long my last name is so let me break it down for you starting with my first name. Pamela, an old fashion name you do not hear often which represents honey and sweetness. A lot of older women have that name so growing up I was the only Pamela I knew. Moving onto my last name Phonxaylinkham. This is derived from Laos and it is defined as power, fortune, and gold. Well, I hope I win the lottery one day other wise, you can can just call me the alphabet soup. Usually people who are from Laos have a long last name, and the reason why my father did not adopt his father's Vietnamese name was for immigration purposes to the United States. My family actually arrived to Ohio first where most of my relatives live and then moved to California.
