Boba

I love to make boba, if you have ever tried it, the balls have a chewy texture and can be sweet. There are a variety you can choose from and it is simple to make it from scratch. I am an avid boba drinker and I hope you can try it someday too!

Fishing

Relaxful time out in nature. Enjoy the company of friends while you catch the feistiest fish out there. I always have a splendid time fishing and don't worry if it's not the legal size we throw it back. There's always plenty of fishes out in the sea (for now).

Snowboarding at China Peak

I love to snowboard every year at China Peak. One of my favorite outdoor hobbies I enjoy during the winter. The adrenaline gets you going and it's fun to go with a group of friends and family.

Horseback Riding Sunset Ranch Hollywood

Would you ever want to try horseback riding on the edge of the cliff while having an overview of the city down below? I did and it's not for the faint of the heart if you are afraid of heights. However, the scenary was beautiful and well worth the price and experience.
