Volleyball is my favorite sport!

No matter if it's on the beach or on the courts, I will play anywhere the ball takes me. I played volleyball throuhgout my whole grade school to high school years. I like to play with friends on my spare time, and my whole family are volleyball players as well. I played setter and libero position, and eventhough my crackling bones don't agree with my joy to play, it is a sport I will never say no to. I did however play other sports such as softball, track, badminton, and basketball but volleyball was my main activity. Give it a try and who knows you might like it too.
