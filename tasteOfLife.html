Boba

I love to make boba, if you have ever tried it, the balls have a chewy texture and can be sweet. There are a variety you can choose from and it is simple to make it from scratch. I am an avid boba drinker and I hope you can try it someday too!

I Love Pizza!

Pizza is my favorite food. If I can eat it everday I would. Pineapple on pizza is the best along with artichoke. A thick stuff crust pizza is perfect.

Dragonfruit

This is my favorite fruit. It is really pricy here in the states, so I would eat it as a treat and savor every bite. There are some white ones too, and its not too sweet but juicy at the same time. Delicious and refreshing to have in the morning.

Fishing

Relaxful time out in nature. Enjoy the company of friends while you catch the feistiest fish out there. I always have a splendid time fishing and don't worry if it's not the legal size we throw it back. There's always plenty of fishes out in the sea (for now).

Snowboarding at China Peak

I love to snowboard every year at China Peak. One of my favorite outdoor hobbies I enjoy during the winter. The adrenaline gets you going and it's fun to go with a group of friends and family.

Horseback Riding Sunset Ranch Hollywood

Would you ever want to try horseback riding on the edge of the cliff while having an overview of the city down below? I did and it's not for the faint of the heart if you are afraid of heights. However, the scenary was beautiful and well worth the price and experience.

Meet Mochi

Maltese Shih Tzu mix. I only had him for a couple of months until I had to move to a new place with no pets allowed and donated him to a loving family. He was precious and I hope to get another in the future.

Meet Sophie

A mixed terrier, we had her for 7 years! She was very kind and loving. Afraid of thunderstorms and cars but a treasure to have.

Candy and Cocoa

Brother and sister, inseparable but the most playful dogs we've ever had. They took a lot of training compared to our other dogs. As you can tell I am a dog lover.

Blackpink

Meet my favorite girl group kpop artist. They are under YG company and consist of 4 members. Our fandom name is called Blinks. They just came out with their new album called The Album, check it out!

I Love EDM!

Electronic dance music that consists of dubstep, trap, house, trance, and hardstyle, you name it! I have an ear for the beats and it always gets me dancing.

I play the flute!

One of my favorite instruments I have been playing since I was 9 years old. I can be a bit rusty now, but it will always come natural to me.

Gundam

One of the first of many that got me into anime. Action packed if you love a good fight scene.

Dragonball Z!

Who doesn't know dragon ball z? This was one of my favorite animes to watch growing up. It never gets old.

Naruto

The anime that my nieces and nephew love to watch. Naruto made ramen very popular actually and the series is still going on till this day.
