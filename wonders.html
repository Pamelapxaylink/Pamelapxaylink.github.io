Machu Picchu

One of the seven wonders of the world and I have always wanted to come here. It is located in Peru and one of the only perserved cities that were not destroyed from the Spanish conquest. Also it is definitely a hidden gem, and I find it amazing how the Inca civilization was able to survive in the mountains.

The Roman Colosseum in Italy

A historical landmark that I would love to visit. It is the biggest amphitheater in the world. Eventhough it was damaged by earthquakes it still stands today. Not only that but centuries of gruesome battles and gladiators were fighting in that same spot. There is even an underground area, and lots of entries. Visiting this site could be an incredible experience.
